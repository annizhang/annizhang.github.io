Home: Inner Mongolia

Film 35mm, Canon AE-1

A series of photographs from May/June 2015 documenting my visit to my grandparents' farm in Northern China, in a rural part of the province of Inner Mongolia. Surrounded by undisrupted natural beauty I chose to present a series that is void of human beings instead focusing on the vibrant colors of the sky and the pristine and wild skylines. This is my favorite place in the world.
