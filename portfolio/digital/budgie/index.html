Budgie Logo and Branding

Adobe Illustrator

For HackCMU Fall 2015 my team created a budgeting app called Budgie for Android. In addition to app development, I was also responsible for the logo design. Since budgie is another name for parakeet, I decided to go with the play on words and I thought the cute bird would be a good presentation for our app.
