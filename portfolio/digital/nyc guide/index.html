personal guide to nyc

Print, Illustrator & Indesign

A book I made as the final project for Communication Design Fundamentals in the spring of 2016. I love city guides but I wanted to make one that had a personal touch since I grew up in New York. I used my own photography and found interesting quotes that to me accurately embodies the spirit of nyc.
