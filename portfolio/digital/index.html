Futura Poster

Adobe InDesign Februrary 2016

I wanted to portray the history of the typeface and demonstrate the historical and artistic background. What stood out to me the most was the Bauhaus influence and the interesting fact that Futura was the first font on the moon. I tried to visualize the efficiency, minimalism, and organization that characterize the typeface and Bauhaus designs. I referenced Bauhaus era paintings and tapestries and I used characteristic colors such as primary reds, blues, yellows, and greens with geometric shapes to show efficiency, orderliness, and to reflect the actual design of the typeface.
