phobia posters

Print, Illustrator & Indesign

A Communication Design Fundamentals assignment in the spring of 2016. Choose 3 phobias to make posters of. After iteration these three are what I came up with. I love sleep, space and food. It was interesting to research about how these three things can be the sources of terror for many people.
