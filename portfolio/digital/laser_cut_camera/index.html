pinhole camera & case

wood, plastic, lasercutter

Final project for Introduction to Rapid Prototyping in Fall 2016. I used Focal Camera's plans and modified the the lens appearance and the ergonomics of the shutter button. I also replaced wooden parts with plastic pieces to create a color motif. I also created a case out of lasercut plastic to keep the wooden panels together and to prevent light leaks.
