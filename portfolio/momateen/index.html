Selfie in Gold

Tape on plastic 3'x 5'

MoMA Teen Program Smash/Cut. Our final individual project invovled first taking a selfie on an iPad, then tracing that selfie onto a transparency and then projecting the transparency onto a large sheet of plastic to create a bigger selfie. Then we used tape to portray our selfies. On dispaly April-May 2014 Museum of Modern Art NYC.

Lady Liberty

Paint on cloth 20' x 5'

This large print was the final group project. With my partner Jessica we created the head holding the phone. We cut out a stencil and used a roller to get it on the cloth. Golden seflies again.
