Chicken Story

Watercolor 3" x 5". Spring 2014

A series of transitions from birth to childhood, to adulthood, and finally to the end. I was shocked by the meat industry's mass production of chicken products and how little it affected consumers. This work was presented in the form of a flip book so the viewer can only see one page at a time, flipping through the first three the viewer sees innocence, and when they come upon the last page it elicits a reaction and hopefully more thought about where their chicken nuggets come from.
